Nikolay N. Zavalishin


PhD in physics and mathematics
(candidate in physical and mathematical sciences)

Senior researcher


e-mail: nickolos@ifaran.ru


My scientific interests include mathematical modeling of ecological systems and processes, bifurcation theory for nonlinear dynamic systems and chaos.

Main research directions:

  • design and analysis of dynamic compartment models for matter and energy cycles in ecosystems,
  • dynamic global vegetation models and reaction of vegetation to the climate change and human perturbations,
  • low-parametric models of global cycles,
  • dynamics and functioning of peatlands,
  • chaos and bifurcations in population models, food webs, trophic chains,
  • nonlinear effects in spatial ecosystem and population models,
  • strange attractors and chaos formation in low-dimensional systems, ...
