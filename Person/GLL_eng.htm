My research efforts have been mainly concentrating in mathematical modelling of ecological systems and processes.

Particular topics have been included:

  • climate change impact on the vegetation;
  • modelling the net primary production;
  • the mathematical models of carbon flux for terrestrial ecosystems;
  • the mathematical models of nitrogen turnover;
  • the mathematical models of methane flux;
  • mathematical models of plant successions;
  • stochastic weather models;
  • dynamic models of the global vegetation pattern.
