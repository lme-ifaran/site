Leonid L. Golubyatnikov


BRIEF BIOGRAPHICAL SKETCH


Born January 22, 1962, in the village of Obraztsovo, Moscow Region, USSR. Graduated from Moscow State University (Mathematics and Mechanics Department) in 1984, with a qualification of mathematician. In 1984-88 I worked in the Research Institute of Precise Apparatus (Moscow) as a mathematician. In 1988 I got a 3-year post-graduate fellowship at the Computer Centre of the USSR Academy of Sciences. In 1991 I entered the Laboratory of Mathematical Ecology, Institute of Atmospheric Physics as a Junior Researcher. The degree of Candidat of Physical-Mathematical Sciences was conferred in 1994 for the thesis entitled "Mathematical Model of Ordination Routine". In 1994-97 I was acting head of the Laboratory of Mathematical Ecology and in 1997-2002 I headed the Laboratory. Since 2002 I have the academic rank of Senior Researcher. See also "Who's Who in the World", 24th ed. Marquis Who's Who, 2007 and later editions; "Who's Who in Science and Engineering" (Marquis Who's Who, 2007 and later editions), "2000 Outstanding Intellectuals of the 21st Century" (International Biographical Centre, Cambridge, England, 2008 and later editions).
