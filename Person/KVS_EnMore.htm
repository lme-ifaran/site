Vladimir S. Kazantsev


BRIEF BIOGRAPHICAL SKETCH


Born May 07, 1986, in Zhukovskiy town, Moscow Region, USSR. Graduated from Moscow State University (Soilscience Department) in 2008, with a qualification of pedologist. In 2008 I got a 4-year post-graduate fellowship at Moscow State University (Soilscience Department). Since 2009 I have worked in the Laboratory of Mathematical Ecology of A.M.Obukhov Institute of atmospheric physics RAS. The degree of Candidat of Biological Sciences was conferred in 2013 for the thesis entitled "Methane Emission from Wetlands of the Northern Region in Western Siberia".
