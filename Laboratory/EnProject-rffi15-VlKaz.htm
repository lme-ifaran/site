Estimating the Methane Flux into the Atmosphere from Southern Tundra Lakes in Western Siberia

Project 16-35-00097 of the Russian Fund of Basic Research
leader Dr. Vladimir S. Kazantsev

Project dates        2016–2017

Information

The project focuses on assessing the contribution of southern tundra lakes in Western Siberia to the atmospheric methane budget. Existing estimates of annual greenhouse gas emissions from the territory under study base on the little data volume from field measurements. In the project, experimental data on methane emissions from tundra lakes in Western Siberia will be obtained.
