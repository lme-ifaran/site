Laboratory
of Mathematical Ecology

 

Our particular topics:

  • mathematical models of carbon flux for terrestrial ecosystems;
  • mathematical models of nitrogen turnover;
  • climate change impact on the vegetation;
  • measurement and modeling of methane fluxes in natural ecosystems;
  • matrix models of age-stage-structured populations;
  • eco-energetic analysis of agroecosystems;
  • stability and bifurcations in dynamic compartment models for ecosystems;
  • dynamic models of the global vegetation pattern;
  • stochastic weather models;
  • special kinds of matrix stability;
  • chain models of plant successions;
  • modelling the net primary production;
  • climate and ecology change impact on urban territories.
