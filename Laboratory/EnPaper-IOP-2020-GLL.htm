ESTIMATION OF C-CO2 BALANCE OF NATURAL STEPPE ECOSYSTEMS: KHAKASSIA AND TUVA (EASTERN SIBERIA, RUSSIA) CASE STUDIES

Golubyatnikov L.L. , Kurganova I.N., Lopes de Gerenyu V.O.

IOP Conference Series: Earth and Environmental Science, vol.606, 012013
doi:10.1088/1755-1315/606/1/012013

Summary

The study of carbon balance of terrestrial ecosystems is considerable important for the forecasts of climate and environmental changes. Using a geoinformation-analytical method and a climate-driven regression model, we have calculated the C-CO2 balance of natural steppe ecosystems of Khakassia and Tuva. Our research has shown that natural steppes of the region under study are a significant sink of carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. The intensity of this carbon flux in the region can be estimate as 184±41 gCm-2yr-1. The annual absorption of carbon dioxide in the natural steppe ecosystems of Tuva and Khakassia is evaluated as 6.9±2.2 Mt C-CO2.
